Knowledge Base
Field references and diagnostic rules of thumb
4-20mA quick read

4 mA = 0% span, 20 mA = 100% span, 12 mA = 50% span. Values below 3.8 mA or above 20.5 mA commonly indicate underrange/overrange or fault signaling depending on device configuration.

Loop load check

Verify supply voltage can drive transmitter, wiring, input resistor, barriers, and accessories. Excessive load can pull the loop low under operating conditions.

Valve mismatch

Compare command against feedback before assuming the valve is stuck. Validate air supply, I/P output, positioner mode, travel calibration, linkage, handwheel, ESD state, and mechanical obstruction.

Telemetry triage

Confirm DC power first, then antenna/feedline, RSSI/noise margin, path obstruction, polling settings, baud/radio config, grounding, and nearby interference sources.

Finding: Diagnostic checks indicate the fault is most likely isolated to [component/subsystem].
